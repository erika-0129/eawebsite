About Erika

Erika Avila is a recent graduate of Florida State University with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science. Originally from Colombia and now based in Miami, she also holds a Bachelor of Arts in Interdisciplinary Studies from Florida International University.

With experience in the administrative and accounting sector, Erika has served as an Administrative and Executive Assistant at The Peebles Corporation, acting as a trusted gatekeeper for high-level executives.

In the summer of 2024, Erika joined Boxraw Labs, a UK based company with a primary focus on sporting apparel but with a vision on revolutionizing the world of boxing thorugh cutting-edge technology and data-driven insights. As an intern, Erika collaborated with the CTO on various data analysis projects using Python as the main coding language.

Although her passion for computers began at a young age, Erika only recently pursued a career in technology. She embraces learning as a lifelong journey and finds joy in expanding her knowledge and skills. Her interests include martial arts, traveling, and photography.
